Anadama Bread

30 Min, 30 Serving

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Boiling water
1 cups Kellogg's® Corn Flake Crumbs
4 cups Kellogg's Corn Flakes® cereal
1/2 cup Molasses
1/3 cup Shortening
1 tablespoon Salt
4 to 4 1/2 cups All-purpose flour
2 packages Active dry yeast
1 Egg
2 tablespoons Butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
1. Measure water and KELLOGG'S Corn Flake Crumbs into large mixing bowl. Stir in molasses, shortening and salt. Let stand until lukewarm.
2. Stir together 1 cup of the flour and yeast. Add to crumbs mixture with egg.
3. Punch down dough. Divide in half. Cover and let rest 10 minutes. Shape each half into round,
4. Bake at 375°F about 20 minutes. Cover with foil to prevent overbrowning and continue
baking about 20 minutes longer .

Apple Crisp

10 Min, 8 Serving

Ingredients:
3/4 cup Kellogg's® Corn Flake Crumbs
1/2 cup All-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 cup Firmly packed brown sugar
1/3 cup Chopped walnuts
1/2 cup Butter or margarine, softened
1 can (1 lb. 4 oz.) apple pie filling
1 tablespoon Lemon juice
1 teaspoon Cinnamon

Directions:
1. For topping, mix together first six ingredients in medium mixing bowl. Set aside.
2. Stir together remaining ingredients in ungreased 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish. Spread evenly in dish. Crumble topping evenly over apples.
3. Bake at 400°F about 30 minutes or until topping is crisp and lightly
browned. Serve warm or cooled with ice cream or whipped topped.
